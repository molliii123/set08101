INSTRUCTIONS:

NAVIGATION:

You can use the buttons shown at the top of all pages when the game is started to navigate! You can return to the main menu, go to the previous story page, save your progress, view the game instructions or quit the game! To view the storylines you have completed, choose the completed storylines button on the main menu!

HOW TO PLAY THE GAME:

To play the game there will be the storyline shown on the top left of each page! you can choose the path you want your character to take with the buttons show at the bottom left of the page! when you reach the end of a storyline you will be shown an end page where you can choose whether to quit the game or start the game again! I hope you enjoy playing the game!
